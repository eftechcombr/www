EF-TECH selects, deploys, and manages open-source solutions for IT asset management and service desk. Our product portfolio combines the flexibility and cost savings of open source with the security and support of a team specialized in containerized infrastructure.

## Our Products

### [GLPI](/en/produtos/glpi/)

GLPI is an open-source IT asset management and ITIL service desk software. EF-TECH offers containerized deployment via Docker and Kubernetes, with managed hosting and dedicated support.

### [WireGuard](/en/produtos/wireguard/)

WireGuard is a modern, containerized, and secure corporate VPN. EF-TECH offers deployment via Docker with Alpine Linux, automated health checks, and production-ready monitoring.
